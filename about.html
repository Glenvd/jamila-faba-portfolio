Jamila Faba is a Cape Town-based creative art director and fashion stylist working across editorial, commercial, and product styling. Her work is defined by intentional composition, cultural depth, and a fearless eye for the unexpected.

With a background spanning print, digital, and campaign work, Jamila collaborates with brands, photographers, and publications across South Africa and internationally to produce imagery that is both visually compelling and deeply considered.
